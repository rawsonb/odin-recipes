Grilled Cheese

Mmm... Grilled cheese

Grilled cheese is easy to make and fun to eat. The tricks to making the best grilled cheese are getting the right crispiness for the bread and using the right type of cheese.

Ingredients

  • Cheddar cheese
  • Sliced bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Spread butter on one side of two slices of bread.
  2. Place first bread slice butter side down, then put slices of cheese on top.
  3. Add the second slice of bread on top of the cheese, butter side up.
  4. Grill the sandwich until the cheese begins to melt, then flip it.
  5. Allow the cheese to fully melt while the other slice of bread is grilled.
  6. Remove the sandwich, turn off the heat, and enjoy.
