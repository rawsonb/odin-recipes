French Toast

Mmm... French toast

French toast is a delicious breakfast food. You can use any type of bread to make it, but denser breads will do better.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs, salt, and milk together.
  2. Butter a pan and put on medium-high heat.
  3. For each bread slice: Dunk bread into the mixture, then place onto pan. Cook for 3-4 minutes on each side.
