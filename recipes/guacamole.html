Guacamole

Mmm... Guacamole

Guacamole is one of the most popular dips in the world for good reason. This recipe is easy to make and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 minced jalapeno pepper
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Mash avocados in a bowl until they are at a consistency you like. (Guacamole consistencies can vary)
  2. Add all other ingredients except salt and mix.
  3. Add salt to taste.
